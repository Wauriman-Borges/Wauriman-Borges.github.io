Welcome to Wauriman Borges Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu

Welcome to the official website of Wauriman Borges Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu, your ultimate destination for Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu enthusiasts.
Our dojo is dedicated to providing a holistic approach to martial arts, focusing on self-improvement, physical fitness, and personal empowerment through the practices of Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu.

Academy and Philosophy

Wauriman Borges Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu, a unique dojo where old school Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu and modern Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu are taught in a pleasant pedagogic instruction by 3rd Degree Black Belt Professor Wauriman Borges, a highly condecorated competitor and instructor. Self discipline, fitness, and self defense are the core of our school, with different level of classes, in a very cosmopolitan environment.

Professor Wauriman proudly formed by Marco Nascimento first black belt of Clube de Jiu-Jitsu Pitbull lead by Master Adilson 'Bitta' Lima , has over 24 years practicing, instructing, and competing.
