The Academy

Welcome to a world-class Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu experience at Wauriman Borges BJJ. Our environment is safe, clean, and enjoyable for enthusiasts of all ages and skill levels. Our team of expert, dedicated instructors, celebrated for their achievements and handpicked for their expertise, are here to guide you towards your goals. With a training approach that's both knowledgeable and engaging, you'll find immense value in learning the art of Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu and Self Defense.

Classes are led by Professor Wauriman Borges, 3rd degree black belt and 2x IBJJF Pan-American champion (2008 and 2022). Professor Borges, a passionate competitor and coach, is equally committed to delivering the highest quality instruction to his students.

Lineage: Mitsuyo Maeda > Carlos Gracie > Helio Gracie > Carlos Gracie Jr. > Adilson 'Bitta' Lima > Marco Nascimento > Wauriman Borges
